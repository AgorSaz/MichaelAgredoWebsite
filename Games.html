Zeia

Endless vertical runner in a ​VR cylindrical environment. In Zeia you play as the almighty wind god, so you will have to help your avatar escape the chamber it was traped in using wind powers controlled in VR before the chamber collapses. Game made in two months.

Armrest

Advanced rehabilitation through Mixed Reality environments for stroke and traumatic brain injury. The system allows training movements that involve all the joints of arms and hands, and also the manipulation of daily objects. In addition, the system monitors body segments and objects and provides rehabilitation exercises through multisensory stimulation that is projected on the area of ​manipulation and interaction.

Game Jams

Fears - 48h

Asymetric VR multiplayer where you play as a kid in his bedroom trying to survive his nightmares at night. As the VR player, you will play against a ghost played by a smartphone with a tracker attached to it.

Genja - 48h

Asymetric VR local multiplayer. In VR, you play as a the security person at a ship factory, where two robots played by gamepads try to escape the facility by gathering all the spaceship parts. You must stop them by controlling cameras and traps at the right time.

Last Way Out - 48h

Survival game with randomly generated hexagon based islands. Survive 100 days in the island without running out of actions while you manage to stay alive. You spend actions for everything you do in the game and recover them by finding places to rest.
